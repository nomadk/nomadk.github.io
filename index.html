Documents ------------- StackEdit stores your documents in your browser, which means all your documents are automatically saved locally and are accessible **offline!** > **Note:** > - StackEdit is accessible offline after the application has been loaded for the first time. > - Your local documents are not shared between different browsers or computers. > - Clearing your browser's data may **delete all your local documents!** Make sure your documents are synchronized with **Google Drive** or **Dropbox** (check out the [ Synchronization](#synchronization) section).
